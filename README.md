# LuminousIndustries
Lucas Bombamba ST10500220
1. Organisation Overview
Founded in 2012, initially focused on sustainable lighting solutions.

Expanded into renewable energy technologies, smart infrastructure, and eco‑friendly manufacturing.

Mission: “To illuminate the world with sustainable technologies that empower communities and protect the planet.”

Vision: “A future where every city shines with clean energy, smart design, and sustainable growth.”

Target audiences: Municipal governments, businesses reducing carbon footprint, global organizations focused on renewable energy.

2. Website Goals and Objectives
Showcase Luminous Industries as an innovative leader; generate qualified leads.

Provide resources such as whitepapers, case studies, and blog posts.

KPIs: Monthly unique visitors, conversion rate, average session duration, newsletter sign‑ups

Proposed Website Features and Functionality
Essential pages: Homepage, About Us, Products/orderform , Contact Page, 

Timeline and Milestones
Weeks 1–2: Requirements gathering, wireframes, design mockups.
Week 3–4: Frontend development (homepage, navigation, core pages)
Week 5–6: Testing test all features  
Week 7: Post-launch review and optimization 
Week 8: regular maintenance
